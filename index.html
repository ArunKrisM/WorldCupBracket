How to play
  • Tap a flag to send it through to the next round. Crown the final winner and the trophy lights up gold with confetti.
  • Press & hold any flag for its profile — nickname, best World Cup, win chance vs its opponent, odds to reach the final and win the cup, a strength slider for "what if", and Show route to trace its path.
  • ▶ Simulate auto-plays the whole bracket · Chalk fills by favourites · Chaos goes wild.
  • ⚡ marks an upset — an underdog beating a stronger side.
  • Undo steps back · Reset clears the board · Share exports an image card to post.
  • 🔇 toggles sound · 🌙 / ☀ toggles dark mode. Your picks live in the link, so a shared URL reopens your exact bracket.
